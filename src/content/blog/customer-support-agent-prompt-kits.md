Prompt kits work best when they are designed around a role, not a model.

For support teams that means grouping prompts by the actual jobs an agent handles: triage, response drafting, refund guidance, escalation summaries, and post-ticket analysis.

When the content on this site covers support prompt kits, the strongest articles will usually include:

- the job to be done
- the inputs required
- the guardrails
- the expected output format
- the tool stack that makes the workflow practical

Those articles naturally open space for affiliate comparisons because readers are often evaluating help desk copilots, AI knowledge tools, QA products, and ticket summarizers at the same moment.
