# Advertising and Sponsorships

AI Prompt Gear is being structured to support future advertising and sponsorship placements, especially on high-intent reference pages where readers are actively evaluating workflow, tooling, and infrastructure decisions.

## Current status

Advertising placements are currently reserved as part of the site layout and information architecture. Their presence does not mean paid advertising is live on every page at this time.

## Commercial placement philosophy

The site is designed around named placement zones so that commercial inventory can be managed consistently as coverage expands. This avoids manually placing ads page by page once the site contains a larger number of categories and reference pages.

## Intended placement types

- Right-rail sponsor placements on documentation pages.
- Footer band placements on deeper reference pages.
- Category-appropriate sponsor inventory on pages with strong research or buying intent.

## Commercial fit

The best future fit is likely to come from:

- Prompt workflow infrastructure.
- Model operations tooling.
- Evaluation and observability software.
- AI workflow platforms or related technical services.

## Transparency

Paid placements should be clearly separated from editorial content and should not silently change how products, vendors, or workflows are evaluated.
