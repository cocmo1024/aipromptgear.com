# Regression Loops

Prompt systems rarely break in dramatic ways. More often they drift. Regression loops exist to catch that drift before a team notices it through lost trust, slower work, or customer-visible mistakes.

## Trigger a recheck when

- A prompt, retrieval source, or policy instruction changes.
- A provider or model version changes behavior.
- A workflow adds or removes a review step.
- Costs or latency targets force routing adjustments.

## Review layers

- Fast spot checks on representative examples.
- Structured regression sets tied to the workflow's highest-risk outcomes.
- Human review of ambiguous cases where scores alone are misleading.
