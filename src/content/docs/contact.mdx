# Contact

Editorial, correction, and business inquiries can be sent to [hello@aipromptgear.com](mailto:hello@aipromptgear.com).

## Best-fit requests

- Correction requests tied to a specific page.
- Business or sponsorship inquiries related to future commercial placements.
- Relevant product briefings or research materials tied to workflow operations, evaluation, tooling, or model infrastructure.
- Partnership conversations that align with the site’s subject matter and audience.

## To help your message get reviewed faster

- Include the page URL when reporting an error or outdated statement.
- Use a clear subject line for sponsorship, affiliate, or editorial requests.
- Explain the practical relevance of any product briefing or announcement.
