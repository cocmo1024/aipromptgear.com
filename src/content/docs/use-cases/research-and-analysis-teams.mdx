# Research and Analysis Teams

Research-heavy teams often benefit from prompt systems when the problem is information scale rather than the absence of judgment. The value comes from faster synthesis, drafting, classification, and comparison support.

## Strong-fit patterns

- Multi-source synthesis and summary drafting.
- Structured comparison work across documents or vendors.
- Internal briefing creation and rapid note consolidation.
- Early-stage information triage before human review.

## Design pressures

- Citation discipline and source traceability matter more than polished wording.
- Human review remains important for interpretation-heavy outputs.
- Tooling should support versioning, evidence capture, and repeatability.
