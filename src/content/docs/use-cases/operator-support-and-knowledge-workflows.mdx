# Operator Support and Knowledge Workflows

Support and internal knowledge workflows are usually the most durable entry point for production prompting. They have steady demand, visible quality signals, and obvious handoff moments between AI output and human accountability.

## Best fit

- Teams responding to repeated inbound questions with a stable knowledge base.
- Internal operations functions that need faster drafting, routing, or summarization.
- Environments where human review is available before final output reaches a customer or employee.

## Design pressures

- Source quality matters more than prompt cleverness.
- Escalation rules must be explicit before automation is expanded.
- Evaluation should focus on factual grounding, actionability, and tone control.
