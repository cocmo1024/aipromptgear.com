# Privacy Notice

This page explains how AI Prompt Gear may collect, use, and protect limited information associated with normal site operation.

Depending on how the site is operated, the following categories of information may be processed:

- Standard request data such as IP address, browser type, device information, and referral source.

Collected information may be used to:

- Maintain site reliability and security.
- Understand which pages and topics are most useful to readers.

The site may use cookies, analytics scripts, or similar technologies to understand performance and usage patterns. If advertising, sponsorship technology, or affiliate tools are introduced later, this notice may be updated to reflect those systems and any relevant third-party data handling.

## Email and direct contact

If you contact the site directly, the information you provide may be retained as needed to respond, review corrections, manage business communication, or maintain an editorial record of the exchange.

## External links

Pages on this site may link to external websites, vendor resources, product pages, or other third-party materials. Those destinations operate under their own privacy practices and are not controlled by this site.

## Data retention

Operational logs, analytics information, and correspondence may be retained only as long as reasonably useful for editorial planning, security, business communication, or legal compliance.

## Policy updates

This notice may be revised as the site grows, especially if analytics, advertising, affiliate programs, or reader accounts are introduced later.
